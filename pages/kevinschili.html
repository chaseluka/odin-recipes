Kevin Malone's Famous Chili

Kevin Malone is an all around kind of guy. His loves include party pack M&M's, American Pie, Roman Noodles, and the number 69. He is famous for his participation as the lead singer and drummer in the bands Scrantonicity and Scrantonicity II. However, the thing Kevin might do best and what he may be most famous for is his world-class chili.
