Kevin Malone's Famous Chili

Kevin Malone is an all around kind of guy. His loves include party pack M&M's, American Pie, Ramen Noodles, and the number 69. He is famous for his participation as the lead singer and drummer in the bands Scrantonicity and Scrantonicity II. However, the thing Kevin might do best and what he may be most famous for is his world-class chili. To learn the secret recipe, follow the instructions below!


Ingredients:

  • 1 pound 85/15 lean ground beef
  • 1 (12 ounces) coarsely chopped yellow onion
  • 1 (15 ounces) can petite diced tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounces) can tomato sauce
  • 2 (about 1 ounce) dried ancho chiles
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Toppings of your choice (shredded cheese, scallions, etc.)

Directions:

  1. Heat up your beef and onions in a large saucepan over medium heat
  2. Brown your beef, but the trick is to undercook your onions
  3. Add your tomatoes, tomato sauce, toasted ancho chilis, and water to the saucepan
  4. Add chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and ground black pepper to taste
  5. Cover the saucepan and simmer your chili for 15 minutes
  6. Add toppings of your choice
